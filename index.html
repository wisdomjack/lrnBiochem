Learn. Memorize. Repeat!

This platform was created to assist students in grasping biochemistry concepts of CHEM 376 class. The material presented here puts an emphasis onto review of the concepts from Gen Chem, Orgo and tricky Math and is designed to enhance general understanding of Biochemistry.

Personal advice, when study biochemistry try to first gain a thorough understanding of the concepts and then memorize them for the exam. In my experience, unfortunately just understanding the concepts is not enough when it comes to the exam. Time is your enemy. By using memorized shortcuts you will have more time to focus on complex problems and will succeed in the long run.
